*My last name is pronounced 'oo-pa-the-ye*

I am a second-year PhD student in the [Language Processing Group](https://langprocgroup.github.io/){:target="\_blank"} at the University of California, Irvine, where I am advised by [Richard Futrell](https://www.socsci.uci.edu/~rfutrell/){:target="\_blank"}.

I am interested in modeling the computational principles and cognitive mechanisms underlying language production using insights and methods from psycholinguistics, information theory, and Natural Language Processing (NLP). 

Before coming to UC Irvine, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Cognitive Science with an emphasis in Machine Learning and Neural Computation and a minor in Math from UC San Diego, where I worked on investigating implicit discourse structure knowledge in neural language models.
